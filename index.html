Hello!

Welcome to my expression page, If you are looking for a strong-willed, independent individual, just expressing themselves unabashedly...then you've come to the right place.

I am a 26 year old part of the common folk born and brought up in Mumbai, the City of Dreams - Oh yeah...I am one of those dreamers of the day. I want to do many things in life - play music, connect with people, explore the world, go out on adventures, romance - the first of which is to find myself.

This so called electronic press kit (EPK) or as I like to call it my "expression kit" is the first step I am taking on the journey to finding myself. Wish me luck!

The Lucid Dream Project

TLD is an independent rock band comprising of 3 talented musician/engineers and I have been playing drums for them for the past 5 years. We have released 2 original compositions and we are 200 subscribers strong.

Do check out our cover video for Choo lo by the Local Train which was made during Covid times to boost people's morale and remember the good times!!!
